Our Buffet Options

With some many options to decide from, let us decide for you!
Through our buffet options which can accommodate up to 20 people and even as little as 2!

Romeo and Juilet

This is our buffet option that serves two people and who else could it be than our star-crossed lovers. Hopefully this won't be your last meal.

Our Fables' Recommendation will be served as a three course meal!!

Seven Dwarfs

This is our buffet option that serves up to 10 people(Couldn't think of a better title). Best for friends and colleagues that want to have some fun and plenty of banter

Depending on the size a variety of Fables' classic dishes will be served including at least three rounds of drinks!

The Last Supper

This is our buffet option that serves up to 20 people. Best for larger gatherings of family, friends and enemies before... you know... the betrayal!

Depending on the size a variety of Fables' classic dishes will be served including at least five rounds of drinks!
